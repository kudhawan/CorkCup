Getting Started
What you Need
  • Table ~ 8 Ft long (or however long your table is), with seating at each end.
  • 2 cups - Rocks glasses are the ideal height and width.
  • 1 Wine Cork.
Gameplay
  • Play to 3 or 5 points.
  • 2v2, 3v3 etc. Teams sit beside each other on opposite ends of the table.
  • Successfully toss the cork into the oppositions cup for a point.
